Business challenge

Traditional banks can feel outdated to graduates, while challenger banks win on perceived relevance and app-first ease.

Target audience

UK graduates, 18+, who finished full-time higher education in the last three years and don't already hold a Barclays student account.

Strategic task

Reframe Barclays from 'established but old' to 'credible, useful and culturally aware' — without losing trust.

Audience & segmentation 06 / 20

Same eligibility criteria. Three different motivations.

The Newly Independent Starter
21–24 · first job or job hunt · moved city

Needs immediate value, switching simplicity and a financial safety net.

The Socially Fluent App-Banker
21–27 · Monzo / Chase / Revolut mindset

Needs proof Barclays can feel as modern as the challengers.

The Progress Planner
24–27 · stable work · future-focused

Needs a bank that feels useful now and credible later.

Shared truth Digitally fluent, time-poor, sceptical of generic bank advertising — and highly responsive to anything that feels recognisably real.

How success will be measured 17 / 20

Not just awareness. Movement.

Brand perception

Lift in 'modern', 'relevant', 'for people like me'.

Engagement

Video completion, swipe depth, creator saves and CTR.

Consideration

Landing-page traffic, offer clicks and eligibility checks.

Conversion

Application starts, completed applications and CPA.

Learning

Test: £175 vs overdraft vs Blue Rewards vs emotional insight.

Every creative expression maps to a measurable behaviour — that's how the campaign stays accountable.

Data, privacy & trust 18 / 20

For a bank, privacy is part of the proposition.

Collect only what helps

Traffic source, content engagement, form progress and consented lead data.

Use it transparently

Optimise media, sequence messages and improve the landing-page journey.

Protect it properly

Clear consent, minimal retention, easy opt-outs and GDPR-compliant handling.

Handled well, trust doesn't slow performance — it strengthens it.

Production notes & AI disclosure 19 / 20

AI was used only for visual conception.

Human-led campaign thinking

All strategy, positioning, segmentation, campaign structure and presentation narrative were developed and arranged for this deck.

AI generated draft visual assets only — then selected, cropped and laid out by hand.

Visual prompts used

Retro-contemporary Instagram carousel using bird's-eye conveyor-belt compositions.

One-page print ad using the same 'adulting struggle' object system.

Supporting backgrounds and scene frames for presentation use.

Included because the brief requires full AI disclosure for imagery and asks that copy and strategy remain human-produced.
