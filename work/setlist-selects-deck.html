Market Analysis · SWOT Setlist Selects · 09

The honest read

Strengths
  • —Deep roster of resident artists across 4 venues
  • —15+ years of genuine live-music heritage
  • —Existing YouTube/Spotify interview format to extend
  • —In-house stages = zero-cost shoot locations
Weaknesses
  • —Artist-led content currently minimal
  • —Four venues can dilute a single brand voice
  • —Short-form production capacity unproven
  • —TikTok presence under-developed vs IG
Opportunities
  • —Short-form rewards authentic, human stories
  • —Artists re-share to their own followings
  • —TikTok search & discovery for new audiences
  • —Repurpose into a wider content ecosystem
Threats
  • —Platform reach & algorithm volatility
  • —Competitors adopting the same playbook
  • —Rising cost pressure on nights out
  • —Artist availability & rights clearance
