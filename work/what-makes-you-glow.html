The Brief

Enlighten, connect, and build lasting loyalty.

MC2002 Creative Advertising brief: develop a four-month campaign strategy for Rhode Beauty (Est. 2022), founded by Hailey Bieber. The goal was to build sentiment and brand loyalty through promotion of wellbeing — a concept central to Rhode’s ethos — by establishing a connection that transcends the transactional brand–consumer relationship.

Rhode’s competitive strengths — Hailey Bieber’s global influence, the “clean girl” aesthetic, affordable luxury positioning, and viral TikTok/Instagram presence — made it uniquely placed for a community-first, values-led campaign. The challenge was to do it without deepening the brand’s over-reliance on its founder.

Market research (Mintel, 2024) showed ~62% of facial skincare users use only 1–2 brands in their daily routine — making retention and brand loyalty the decisive competitive lever.

Campaign Strategy

Shifting Rhode from a product to an experience.

“What Makes You Glow?” centres itself around the everyday moments that light individuals up inside. Hailey Bieber kicks off the campaign by sharing her own personal “glow moments,” leveraging her connections with notable figures to set the tone — then handing the platform to the community to do the same.

By focusing on the small, joyful moments that contribute to inner and outer radiance, the campaign shifts Rhode’s position from a luxury skincare brand to an experience — a partner that helps people feel their best for the moments that matter most.

  • Build community-driven narratives that celebrate authenticity and individuality
  • Drive engagement through earned brand loyalty rather than bought attention
  • Position Rhode as a brand that understands beauty’s relationship to mental wellness
Product Focus

Peptide Glazing Fluid & Barrier Restore Cream.

The campaign centres Rhode’s hydrating barrier-repairing hero products — framed not as skincare items but as tools that prepare and enhance your natural glow for your best and worst days alike.

New for the campaign: limited-edition Glow Kits — curated product bundles in exclusive minimalist skincare pouch packaging directly inspired by the campaign’s theme. Designed to be shareable, giftable, and inherently social-first.

Execution

Social. Experiential. Creator-led. Charitable.

  • Hero campaign video — Hailey Bieber and a diverse group sharing their “glow moments,” paired with Rhode products. Distributed on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Rhode’s website.
  • #WhatMakesYouGlow challenge — TikTok hashtag challenge encouraging users to share short videos about the little things that make them glow. Instagram polls, TikTok trends and filters simulating the Rhode “glazed” look amplify participation.
  • Pop-up events — immersive activations in New York, LA, and London offering mini facials, interactive installations and “What Makes You Glow” reflection moments — drawing on the success of Rhode’s Lip Case pop-ups.
  • Virtual Glow Workshops — live sessions hosted by Hailey alongside skincare professionals, educating on radiant skin and connecting glow to self-care and confidence.
  • Inclusive brand trip — an all-inclusive influencer trip built to actively combat the homogeneity criticism faced by brands like Tarte, with intentional creator diversity across backgrounds and demographics.
Social Impact

Beauty that gives back.

To deepen the campaign’s emotional resonance, Rhode partners with three charitable organisations aligned to its wellbeing ethos: NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness), Active Minds, and The Trevor Project — supporting mental health and self-esteem in young people.

This initiative reinforces the campaign’s core message: that glow comes from within, and is nurtured by both self-love and community care. Research (MIT Sloan, 2021) confirms that charitable brand positioning drives both consumer sentiment and commercial performance.

Timeline

Four months. Three stages.

Pre-Launch — Weeks 1–6
  • Cryptic social teasers: “What Makes You Glow?”
  • BTS with Hailey + the Rhode team
  • Influencer seeding with personalised packages
  • Email teasers to existing mailing list
Rollout — Weeks 6–10
  • Hero campaign video across all platforms
  • #WhatMakesYouGlow hashtag challenge
  • Limited-edition Glow Kit product launch
  • Influencer activation across demographics
Measurement — Weeks 10–12
  • Pop-up events in NY, LA, London
  • Virtual workshops with Hailey + experts
  • UGC closing reel: best #WhatMakesYouGlow
  • Impact newsletter to Rhode community
